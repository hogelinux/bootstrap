Japanese in Cebu

I think that Japanese people in Cebu live by extending the Japanese community.
We tend to go out with Japanese and talk in Japanese.
On the other hand, Koreans and Taiwanese people often talk to the local people in English.
Even Japanese who have high English skills, when talking to local people they don't speak much and talk in short sentences.
When Koreans and Taiwanese speak with locals, they can talk very well, laugh at each other, and enjoy the conversation.
Japanese students talk often when talking to teachers who have become friends,
but they are afraid to speak with the people who are meeting for the first time or the local people.
Because The Japanese are growing up with a Japanese cultur that is shy and withhold.
